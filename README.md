# PulseIQ

### Transforming Operational Chatter into Business Intelligence

PulseIQ is an AI-powered operational intelligence platform designed for SMEs, manufacturers, traders, and distributors.

Businesses generate hundreds of operational updates every week across production, sales, procurement, dispatch, and finance teams. Critical business knowledge often remains buried inside chats, emails, and status updates, making it difficult for owners to track issues, understand operational health, and make informed decisions.

PulseIQ converts unstructured employee updates into structured business events, builds entity-centric timelines, and enables business owners to ask natural language questions and receive trustworthy, evidence-backed answers.

Instead of acting as a traditional chatbot, PulseIQ serves as an Operational Memory Layer for the business—preserving observations, constructing business facts, and generating insights grounded in evidence.

## Key Capabilities

* Multi-tenant architecture for multiple businesses
* Natural language employee updates
* AI-powered event and entity extraction
* Immutable business event store
* Entity timeline generation
* Hybrid retrieval (SQL + Semantic Search)
* Source-backed answers with confidence scores
* Auditability and trust-first design
* Operational intelligence for manufacturers, traders, and distributors

## Example Questions

* What is pending this week?
* Any issues with Rajan Traders?
* How was Line 2 this week?
* Which orders were delayed?
* Which payments are still outstanding?
* What operational risks should I be aware of?

## Core Design Principle

Messages are observations.

Events are business facts.

The LLM is a reasoning layer—not the source of truth.

Trust is built through evidence, timelines, and auditability.
